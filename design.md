# Premier Design - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Language
**Sophisticated Minimalism**: Clean, uncluttered layouts that let the furniture be the hero. Every element serves a purpose, with generous white space creating breathing room and emphasizing quality over quantity.

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Pure White** (#FFFFFF) - Main background, clean and premium
- **Warm White** (#FAFAFA) - Section backgrounds, subtle differentiation
- **Charcoal** (#2C2C2C) - Primary text, strong contrast

**Accent Colors:**
- **Terracotta** (#C4704A) - Brand accent, call-to-action buttons, highlights
- **Soft Beige** (#F5F2ED) - Secondary backgrounds, card backgrounds
- **Light Gray** (#E8E8E8) - Borders, subtle dividers

**Supporting Colors:**
- **Success Green** (#4A7C59) - Success states, availability indicators
- **Warning Amber** (#D4A574) - Attention, limited availability

### Typography
**Primary Font**: "Playfair Display" - Elegant serif for headings and brand elements
**Secondary Font**: "Inter" - Clean, modern sans-serif for body text and UI elements

**Hierarchy:**
- **H1**: Playfair Display, 48px, Bold - Main headings
- **H2**: Playfair Display, 36px, Semibold - Section headings
- **H3**: Inter, 24px, Semibold - Subsection headings
- **Body**: Inter, 16px, Regular - Main content
- **Small**: Inter, 14px, Regular - Captions, metadata

## Visual Effects

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and element animations
- **Splide.js**: Elegant image carousels and product galleries
- **ECharts.js**: Clean data visualization for company statistics
- **p5.js**: Subtle background effects and creative elements
- **Matter.js**: Physics-based interactions for product cards
- **Pixi.js**: Advanced visual effects and image processing
- **Shader-park**: Sophisticated background gradients and textures

### Animation & Styling
**Hero Section Effects:**
- Subtle parallax scrolling on hero images
- Gradient text animation on main headings
- Smooth fade-in animations for content sections

**Interactive Elements:**
- 3D tilt effects on product cards with shadow expansion
- Color morphing buttons with terracotta accent transitions
- Smooth hover states with elegant scaling effects

**Background Treatments:**
- Consistent soft gradient background throughout the site
- Subtle texture overlays for depth without distraction
- Clean geometric shapes as decorative elements

### Header Effect
**Navigation Bar:**
- Fixed transparent header with blur backdrop
- Smooth color transition on scroll
- Elegant typography with proper spacing
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu

### Product Showcase
**Card Design:**
- Clean white cards with subtle shadows
- Terracotta accent borders on hover
- High-quality furniture photography
- Consistent aspect ratios and spacing

**Gallery Effects:**
- Smooth image transitions with fade effects
- Infinite scroll carousels for product collections
- Zoom-on-hover for detailed product views

### Responsive Design
**Mobile-First Approach:**
- Optimized touch interactions
- Simplified navigation for small screens
- Proper image scaling and compression
- Fast loading times across all devices

## Brand Consistency
All visual elements maintain the sophisticated, premium aesthetic that reflects Premier Design's commitment to quality craftsmanship and elegant design solutions.
